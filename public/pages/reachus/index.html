HOW TO REACH VARAZZE

Varazze is located in the heart of the Riviera di Ponente , in a strategic position close to Milan, Turin , Genoa and the major cities of central Europe. The Marina di Varazze is immersed in the greenery, connected to the heart of the characteristic town of Varazze by a walk which can be covered on foot in just five minutes, it can count on a privileged position: twenty five kilometers separate it from Genoa and its international airport Cristoforo Colombo, one hundred and fifty of the motorway from Milan and turin. In less than two and a half hours by car you reach the border with Switzerland. Varazze is, therefore, among the tourist centers of Ponente Ligure more quickly and comfortably reachable even on the occasion of crowded weekends as well as for traditional holiday stays, served, among other things, by the railway stations of Varazze and Savona.
