Frequently Asked Questions
Why are you doing these events in Varazze?

Varazze is hit by a serious flooding disaster. A lot of people is suffering because of this. Some of them lost everything. It's a brutal destiny, but with just some few money you can help people go back to their normal life.

How can I apply an event?

You can add an event to your cart whenever you want, then you can buy it. But first, you need to create an account.

Login and register an account on VarazzersFestival

In the navbar, click on the icon image and then on "Login". If you don't have an account yet, you can create one in just 1 minute! It's simple and if you don't remember the password, you can click on the "stay logged" button.

How to contact event organizers?

I recommend you to contact the event organizers directly for invitation, visa, paper submission and ticketing related inquiries.

How can I reach Varazze?

Click on the "reach us" button at the bottom of the page. I explain anything you want to know about Varazze and how to reach this beautiful countryside town.
